Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how HRSoftware ("we", "us", or "our") handles information in connection with the MechRef mobile and desktop application ("the App").

MechRef does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. The App functions entirely on-device and does not require an account or internet connection to operate.

Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect any of the following:

· Personal identification information (name, email, address, phone number)
· Device identifiers or hardware information
· Location data
· Usage analytics or crash reports
· Browser or search history
· Any other personally identifiable information

In-App Purchases

MechRef offers optional in-app purchases to unlock premium features. All payment transactions are processed exclusively by Google Play (Android) or Microsoft Store (Windows). We do not receive, process, or store any payment or billing information. Please refer to the privacy policies of Google and Microsoft for details on how they handle transaction data.

Internet Access

The App may use an internet connection solely to verify in-app purchase entitlements through the platform store (Google Play / Microsoft Store). No personal data is transmitted during this process. All technical reference content (thread tables, calculators, engineering data) is stored locally on your device.

Third-Party Services

MechRef does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs. No data is shared with third parties.

Children's Privacy

MechRef does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age. Since the App collects no personal data at all, it is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
